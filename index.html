Introduction

Welcome to BlogRack. We're a simple blogging platform that allows users to register, login, and share their thoughts through blog posts. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Password (stored securely in hashed format)

When you post blogs, we store:

  • The content of your posts
  • Publication dates and times
  • Edit history (if you modify your posts)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Allow you to publish and manage blog posts
  • Display your content to other users
  • Send important service notifications
  • Improve our platform based on usage patterns

Content Moderation

All blog posts are automatically scanned for policy violations. Our system will automatically remove content that violates our guidelines.

Automated Moderation

We use automated systems to scan all posts for inappropriate content, hate speech, spam, and other policy violations before they're published.

In some cases, we may also manually review content when:

  • A post is reported by other users
  • Our automated systems flag potential issues
  • We need to investigate potential violations

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms
  • All data transmissions are encrypted (HTTPS)
  • Regular security audits of our systems
  • Limited access to personal data

Important Note

While we implement security measures, no online service is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and don't share your login credentials.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Edit your blog data
  • Unpublish your blog
  • Delete your account and associated data by mailing us

Data Export

You can export all your blog posts at any time through your account settings in Markdown format.
